Welcome to Laundry Wallah / Laundry Mart.

A laundry mart is a type of retail store that specializes in providing laundry services to customers who want to wash and dry their clothes. Laundry mart stores typically have largeers on-site washers and dryers that are available for customers to use, and offer a variety of wash cycle options and clothing care products to choose from. Some laundry mart stores may also offer additional services such as stain removal, fabric softener application, and folding and packaging of clothes. These stores can be found in a variety of locations and may be operated as standalone businesses or as part of larger retail chains.
